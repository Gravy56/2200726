What's Inside

Only honest materials

01

Certified Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified, grown without pesticides or synthetic fertilizers. Breathable, durable, and irresistibly soft — every layer is covered in it.

02

Natural Latex (GOLS)

Sustainably tapped from Hevea rubber trees in small batches. Naturally resistant to mold, dust mites, and bacteria — with buoyant, pressure-relieving support.

03

Joma & Merino Wool

Ethically sourced, naturally flame-resistant, and moisture-wicking. Keeps you cool in summer, warm in winter — no chemical treatments required.

04

Tempered Steel Pocket Coils

Individually wrapped, carbon-neutral steel coils that move independently to minimize motion transfer and provide targeted spinal support.
