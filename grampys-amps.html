The Man Behind the Mojo

David Vomacka

David has spent decades chasing tone — disassembling, diagnosing, and resurrecting the vintage tube amplifiers that defined rock and roll. What started as a passion for the gear his grampy played has become a life's work restoring classics for players and collectors across the country.

Every amp that comes through the shop is treated with the reverence it deserves. Original components are preserved wherever possible. Transformers are rebuilt. Cabinets refinished. And when it's done, it sounds better than the day it left the factory.

How It Works

Process & FAQ

Clear process, realistic timelines, and no surprise work. Final pricing is confirmed after bench diagnosis.

1) Initial Contact

Send model, year, symptoms, and photos. You'll get a rough range and next steps for drop-off or shipping.

2) Bench Diagnosis

A full technical assessment confirms parts, labor, and turnaround before restoration begins.

3) Approval & Restore

Work is performed only after approval. Originality is preserved whenever possible, with any mods discussed first.

4) Test & Return

Final burn-in and play test before pickup or return shipping.
