about

WHO WE ARE

The AITE Computer Science Club is a student-run organization at the Academy of Information, Technology & Engineering — a STEAM-focused magnet high school serving students from across Fairfield County, Connecticut.

We bring together students at every skill level to build projects, compete in competitions, and explore the cutting edge of technology — from web dev to machine learning to low-level systems programming.

AITE gives us an incredible foundation: AP Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Project Lead the Way pathways, and a 1:1 laptop program. We take that further, on our own terms, after the bell rings.
