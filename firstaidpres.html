Skill 01
CPR — Cardiac Arrest

Brain death starts in 4 minutes. Every 30 seconds without CPR cuts survival by 10%.

The Ratio
30 compressions, 2 breaths — then repeat
Depth & Speed
2 inches deep · Stayin' Alive is exactly 100 BPM
Rescue Breaths
Head tilt — chin lift — seal — 1 second breath — chest should rise
Use the AED
Turn it on. It will tell you exactly what to do. Don't be scared of it.
Skill 02
Severe Bleeding

A severed artery can kill in 3 minutes. Pressure is the weapon.

Direct Pressure
Press hard. Don't peek. Don't lift the cloth.
Add more on top if it soaks through.
Tourniquet
Limb bleeds that won't stop. 2–3" above wound. Tighten until bleeding stops. Write the time.
Wound Packing
Deep wounds — push gauze in, pack it tight, then press. Don't pull it out.
Common Mistakes
What to do —
and what not to
DO
✓
Stay on scene — don't move the victim unless they're in immediate danger
✓
Keep them warm — shock causes dangerous drops in body temp, even in summer
✓
Talk to them — even if they're unconscious, calm reassurance matters
✓
Keep pressure on — do not lift cloth to check, ever. Add more on top.
✓
Recovery position — unconscious but breathing? On their side, airway clear
✓
Tell EMS everything — time of injury, what happened, what you did
DON'T
✗
Don't give food or water — especially if they might need surgery
✗
Don't remove embedded objects — a knife or stick in a wound is plugging it
✗
Don't put ice on burns — use cool running water for 20 minutes, not ice
✗
Don't straighten a broken bone — immobilize it exactly as you find it
✗
Don't let a tourniquet go once on — only EMS removes it
✗
Don't freeze — doing something imperfect is always better than doing nothing
