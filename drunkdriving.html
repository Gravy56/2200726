Last

Ethyl alcohol is a central nervous system depressant. Each drink slows the brain a little more — judgment, reaction time, and spatial awareness degrade with every glass.

Impaired
Behind the Wheel

At 0.08% BAC — the legal limit — a driver's reaction time is doubled. Peripheral vision narrows. Lane-keeping becomes unreliable. The driver usually feels fine.

88,000
Deaths a Year

Alcohol is the 5th-leading preventable cause of death in the United States. One person dies in a drunk-driving crash every 45 minutes.

Alcohol & the Human Body

50–60% genetic risk of addiction. Reduces life expectancy by 10 years. Linked to 7 types of cancer. Withdrawal can be fatal without medical supervision.
